Cyber Threat Handling

Endpoint Detection and Response 24/7

Monitoring and collecting data regarding endpoint activity that may suggest the presence of a threat, then analyze this data to discover threat pattern

Identify and respond to any impersonation and infringement

Recognizing impersonations and infringements of our client organization's name, trademark, logo, slogan or text and responding to them in a legally approved and properly designed method.

Predictions of cyber threats on social media

We take advantage of characteristics obtained from hacker social networks and online providers of cybersecurity bulletins. We approach the topic of forecasting the level of vulnerability of social media flaws by mining hacker social networks for qualities that can be used as predictors of future social media attacks.

Handling social media hacking threats

Handling social media hacking threats by enforcing all necessary security measures and predicting threats beforehand so as to take necessary security measures before an attack while patching up vulnerabilities through a 24/7 process of monitoring.

Handling private messages

Filtering private messages received to the organization's social media accounts in order to secure the account/s from phishing attacks and other cyber threats.

Fixing reputational damage

Fixing reputational damage by using suitable counter measures recommended by the cyberacker crisis response team.

Digital Forensic Report

Generation of a fully analyzed digital forensic research report once a cyber crime is caused which would include how the crime has taken place while also discovering and reporting the parties involved in the crime.

Provision of social media reports

Generation of social media reports which includes any social media threat activity or other account vulnerabilities of a certain period of time(every fortnight).
