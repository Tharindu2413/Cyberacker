Privacy Policy

At CYBERACKER, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our clients' personal and sensitive information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information related to our clients' use of our digital solutions, cyber security, and web development services.

Information We Collect
We collect information from our clients when they use our services. This information includes personal and contact information, such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, and physical addresses. We may also collect technical information, such as IP addresses, browser type, operating system, and device information. Additionally, we may collect information about our clients' use of our services, including the types of services accessed and the frequency and duration of use.
Use of Information
We use the information we collect to provide our digital solutions, cyber security, and web development services to our clients. We may use personal information to communicate with clients, to provide support and assistance, to invoice and process payments, and to improve our services.

We may also use technical information to analyze and improve the functionality of our services, to troubleshoot technical issues, and to prevent and detect fraud and other unauthorized activity.
Disclosure of Information
We do not sell, rent, or share our clients' personal information with third parties, except as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. We may share information with our partners, contractors, or service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as payment processing, website hosting, and data analysis.

We may also disclose information when required by law or when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights or the rights of our clients, to comply with a legal process, or to respond to a government request.
Data Security
We take reasonable measures to protect the security and integrity of our clients' personal and sensitive information. We implement physical, technical, and administrative safeguards to prevent unauthorized access, use, or disclosure of information. However, we cannot guarantee the security of information transmitted over the internet, and clients should take their own precautions to protect their information.
Updates to Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will post the updated policy on our website and notify clients of any material changes. We encourage our clients to review this policy periodically to stay informed about our practices regarding privacy and security.
Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at info@cyberacker.lk .
We remain at the forefront of technological advances and industry best practices, ensuring that our clients receive the most up-to-date and effective solutions available.
